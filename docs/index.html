Commands

.join

Join voice channel

Aliases : on

.leave

Leave voice channel

Aliases : off

.play <URL | search term>

Play music or add music to queue

Aliases : p

.playnext <URL | search term>

Play music or add music to the top of queue

.stop

Stop music

.pause

Pause music

.resume

Resume music

.skip

Skip to next music in queue if queue exists

.remove <Number>

Remove music in queue at index if queue exists

Aliases : rm

.clear

Clear music in queue

.repeat <none | one | all>

Disable repeat music | repeat current music | repeat all music in order of queue

.volume <Number>

Adjust volume of voice activity between 0 and 100. does not the user-designated volume of the client itself

.save

Save current song list to an online database

.load

Load song list from an online database to queue. automatically plays if there is no voice activity
